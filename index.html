About Me

My name is Sita Robinson. I am a Sophomore Data Science major at Drexel University with a minor in Computer Science. I am expected to graduate with my Bachelors degree in 2021. I am a treasurer and member of the Women in Computing Society (WiCS). Other professional organizations I am a member of are Society of Women Engineers (SWE) and IEEE at Drexel University. In the summer of 2017, I was a Software Engineering Intern at Comcast in Philadelphia. Some of my hobbies include programming, photography, playing the clarinet, playing Scrabble and traveling around the world. I am originally a Northern Virginia/Metro D.C. native.
